# Content Style Guide

## Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Confident without being arrogant
- Technical when needed, clear always

## Language
- Primary: English (for international audience)
- Secondary: Russian (for local projects)

## What to Emphasize
- Practical hands-on experience
- Problem-solving abilities
- BIM + Programming intersection (rare combo)
- Automation and efficiency improvements

## What to Avoid
- Overused buzzwords ("passionate", "guru", "ninja")
- Vague claims without context
- Overly formal corporate speak

## Writing Rules
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise
- Show, don't tell (examples > claims)
- Quantify achievements when possible
